# Sleep Basics: Small Changes That Work

A simple checklist for better sleep without overhauling your life.

## The 5-point checklist
- **Wake time**: keep it within a 60–90 minute window.
- **Morning light**: 5–15 minutes outside within 1 hour of waking.
- **Caffeine cutoff**: stop 8 hours before bed.
- **Wind-down**: 20 minutes of low stimulation (dim, quiet, simple).
- **Bed = sleep**: if you can’t sleep after ~20–30 minutes, reset (leave bed briefly).
