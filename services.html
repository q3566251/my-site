How it works

Simple, clear, and designed around your life.

1

Free 15-min consult

We talk about what's going on, what you're hoping for, and whether we're a good fit. No pressure.

2

First full session

We go deeper — your history, patterns, and goals. Together we outline a clear initial plan.

3

Ongoing sessions

Weekly or biweekly 50-minute sessions. Skills, reflection, and real progress between appointments.

4

Progress check-ins

We regularly review what's working and adjust — so therapy actually moves forward.

Length: 50 minutes
Frequency: Weekly or biweekly
Format: Telehealth or in-person (where available)

Who we work with

Adults and young adults navigating anxiety, burnout, life change, relationships, and self-esteem. Culturally responsive, trauma-informed, and non-judgmental.

Common questions

Answers to what people often wonder before starting.

How do I know which service is right for me?

You don't have to figure that out alone. The free consult is specifically designed to help us understand what you're going through and match you with the right focus area.

Can I switch focus areas mid-therapy?

Absolutely. As your goals evolve, we adjust. Therapy is flexible — it follows your life, not a rigid template.

Do you offer telehealth?

Yes. Telehealth sessions are available depending on your location and applicable regulations. Many clients prefer the flexibility.

How long does therapy usually take?

It depends on your goals and depth of work. Some prefer 8–12 focused sessions; others benefit from longer engagement. We'll discuss this in your first session.

Do you take insurance?

Add your insurance policy details here — in-network, out-of-network, or superbill options.

What if I've never been to therapy before?

That's completely okay. Many clients are new to therapy. We'll take it at your pace, explain the process, and make sure you feel comfortable every step of the way.

Ready to take the first step?

A 15-minute consult is free, low-pressure, and a good way to see if we're the right fit for you.
