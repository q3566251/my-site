Sleep Basics: Small Changes That Work

2026-03-06 · tags: sleep, routine

Better sleep usually comes from consistency, light exposure, and a calmer pre-sleep routine—not perfection.

The 5-point checklist

  • Wake time: keep it within a 60–90 minute window.
  • Morning light: 5–15 minutes outside within 1 hour of waking.
  • Caffeine cutoff: stop 8 hours before bed (adjust if needed).
  • Wind-down: 20 minutes of low stimulation (dim, quiet, simple).
  • Bed = sleep: if you can’t sleep after ~20–30 minutes, reset (leave bed briefly).
Track what changes sleep the most for you: light, caffeine timing, and bedtime consistency often have the biggest impact.

睡眠基础：小改变也能真正有效

2026-03-06 · 标签：睡眠、作息

睡眠变好通常来自：稳定节律、光照管理、以及更安静的睡前流程——而不是“完美睡眠”。

五步清单（最有效的核心）

  • 起床时间：尽量控制在 60–90 分钟的浮动范围内。
  • 早晨见光：起床后 1 小时内到户外 5–15 分钟。
  • 咖啡因截止：睡前 8 小时停止摄入（按个人敏感度调整）。
  • 降刺激收尾：睡前 20 分钟低刺激（调暗、安静、简单）。
  • 床只做一件事：若 20–30 分钟仍睡不着，短暂离开床位“重启”，再回来。
你可以观察哪一项最影响你：通常“早晨见光”“咖啡因时间”“睡眠节律稳定”三项收益最大。
